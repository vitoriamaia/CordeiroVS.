YITH WooCommerce Bulk Product Editing allows you to edit the prices of different products at the same time.


== Description ==

Editing a large amount of items can be extremely annoying: you have to select them one by one, choosing the value you want. With YITH WooCommerce Bulk Product Editing, you will be free to apply price modifications to a great quantity of items, applying bulk actions to increase or decrease the value, or setting a new price for every selected item. A simple and direct way to fulfill a long and stressful operation with few clicks!


= Features: =

* Possibility to filter your products by category, regular price, and sale price before editing
* A specific table to edit prices of the products before saving values
* Possibility to edit prices of multiple products, setting them equal to a specific value, or increasing/decreasing them by a value or a percentage

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `YITH WooCommerce Bulk Product Editing` from Plugins page

YITH WooCommerce Bulk Product Editing will add a new submenu called "Bulk Product Editing" under "YIT Plugins" menu. Here you are able to edit your products.
